The ontology about everything related to football!

Football Information Ontology (FIO) is an ontology born from the observation of the media expansion regarding the sport of football with particular regard to the betting systems attached to this. Since football is one of the most known and loved sports in the world, we want to contribute to give the possibility to know the latest developments and innovations occurred in this environment using automatic reasoning systems. This platform offers services to allow you to know the latest news and status of what is happening in the professional, sporting and also the organization of the latest football matches.
